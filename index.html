Teckade leaving you in the past?

There is a global pandemic of underskilled workers having little to no digital skills. This affects entry level to managerial positions. AI and robots replacing jobs in the current socially distanced world but we are bridging the teck-related skills gap by teaching the unskilled how to survive in the new teckade. We are preparing the youth for the teckade. Technology is being integrated into every aspect of life but the school system lags behind and underdeveloped countries lack the resources to keep up. Teckade allows the highly skilled to pull up the underskilled.
